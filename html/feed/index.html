Be a Global Citizen, Care for everyone & start from your neighborhood!!! – Something from my heart…

Today in the mid of Global Crisis, Supermarkets staff, Security Guards, Cleaners, Strata people, Bin truck drivers, Aged Care workers, Disability workers, Nurses, Doctors, Police Cops, Public Transport workers, Food/necessity carriers, drivers, cabbies, Emergency workers, Childcare workers, Airport workers, Teachers, Moms, Dads, Sister, Brothers, Children, friends are our new Heroes!!

Respect everyone for risking their lives for us and supporting our lives & livelihood!! Instead, we are giving them anger, agony. They are doing their jobs & are saviours!!

Whenever you see anyone of these, please respect and ask them for water or coffee or food. They have been working 24/7 and mostly without any time for basic nourishment.

Multiculturalism & Respect for all is what I learnt by being an Australian!!

Let’s carry that spirit of care to everywhere around the world!!

If you have any elderly person or any self-isolating family living next to you, just focus on caring, supporting & helping only 2 families – the ones living next door to your left & right. Get them groceries, food, supplies when you’re getting for yours.

Get flowers for them today and say it – we’re there & post pics, so others also do. I’m doing it today!!!

These small efforts can create a Human Chain of care, affection & confidence in the community that come what may, we can stand against anything, because, we are together & we’re not mere Indians, Asians, Pakistanis, Chinese, Koreans, Australians, Sikhs, Hindus, Christians, Muslims…

We are Global Citizens & we care for everyone!!

GD Singh, Chief Executive Officer, Apply4Study
